Power Puff Girls Information

Bubbles

Some of Bubbles' unique special abilities are Superhuman Strength and X-ray Vision!

Blossom


Blossom is the leader of the group and in the earlier episodes, she discovers
she has Ice Breath and Laser Beam Eyes!

Buttercup


Buttercup is the most unique in terms of personality because
she has a cool, tomboy-like personality that makes her different from the other girls.
Let's also not forget her strong powers which are Fireball and Ball Blast!
